Project Name: Dr Drago's Madcap Chase (fan remake)

Concept: A digital board game inspired by the classic Amiga game, Dr. Drago's Madcap Chase. The goal is for players to race across a stylized map to reach a final destination first.

Core Game Mechanics:
- Game Board: A network of interconnected "nodes" on a map. Nodes can be cities, event locations, car dealers, or plain travel spaces.
- Player Movement: Movement is determined by a random roll (dice) to move a set number of nodes per turn.
- Economic System: Players manage money to buy new cars or repair damage. They can also buy "shares" in cities for passive income.
- Dr. Drago NPC: A non-player character who moves randomly and penalizes players on the same node.
- Character Traits: Each playable character has a unique passive ability.
